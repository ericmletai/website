How I See It

There's always a threshold between what a person feels and what a system understands. I believe good design lives exactly there — the space where human behavior and intelligent systems have to meet halfway.

Designer's job isn't getting smaller — it's getting more human. Someone has to hold the line between what machines can do and what people actually need. That's the work I show up for.
